About Me


Introduction


  As an electronics-turned-computer engineering student, my journey has taken me from learning about hardware to diving into software and problem-solving. Building a strong foundation in engineering knowledge and skills, I am driven to make a positive impact using technology. Recent advancements in 5G networks, Artificial Intelligence, Virtual Reality, Semiconductors tie into Multi-Access Edge Computing and I'm curious to see how it's going to shape our connected world. I am genuinely excited about the future, as I anticipate the boundless possibilities they bring to The Internet of Things.

Through various projects, I've continued honing my programming skills and cultivating a detail-oriented approach to problem-solving. I firmly believe that learning and growing in software development is necessary, which fuels my curiosity and compels me to consistently grow my skills. Beyond academics, I actively contribute to the student community, serving on the executive board of the IEEE Student Branch at ASU. In addition, during my bachelor's degree, I also held a position on the board of IET NITK branch, and I even had the pleasure of representing the NITK Basketball team.

But hey, life isn't just about academics and work, right? When I am not buried in code or studying, you will find me hitting the gym, exploring hiking trails, and engaging my mind with an intriguing book each month. I also occasionally indulge myself in japanese anime and manga for some alluring stories and artwork.

I am thrilled to share my projects and experiences with you, so please feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out to me. Let us connect and embark on a remarkable journey together!
